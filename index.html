The Consortium

A Unified Pan-African Intellectual Network

The U6 Plus consortium is a collaboration of eleven universities spread across Africa: Cape Peninsula University of Technology; University of Eldoret, Kenya; Kwara State University, Nigeria; The University of The Gambia; University of Cape Coast, Ghana; University of Ilorin, Nigeria; University of Abuja, Nigeria; The Federal University of Technology, Nigeria; Al-Hikmah University, Nigeria; Vaal University of Technology, South Africa, and the University of Calabar, Nigeria.

Read Full Profile

2026 Context

Driving AU Agenda 2063 through 5IR

The role of the universities in Africa is key to realizing the millennium development goals as well as African Union Agenda 2063 – The Africa We Want. As such, the U6+ Consortium, under the auspices of the University of Eldoret in Kenya, shall organize an international conference in September, 2026 focusing on inclusive innovations and citizen science.
